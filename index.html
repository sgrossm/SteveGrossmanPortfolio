About Me

Exploring the intersection of creativity and logic.

Growing up, I was exposed to musicians and engineers who kindled my growing passion for music and technology. My father, a fantastic keyboard player and electrical engineer found his middle ground building modular synthesizers. I spent much of my youth playing instruments, composing digital music, and creating illustrations. After graduating from the University of California, Irvine, with a degree in Computer Science, I took an Information Security software engineering role. This position fascinated me because it applied real-world concerns to application development. I learned firsthand how to balance software functionality with potential business threats. After a few years, I began searching for a career that could combine technical expertise with creative expression. Playing some of my favorite games again (Oddworld, Chrono Trigger, Cuphead, Bioshock) led me to realize game development is the perfect convergence of my interests. I started to dive into the world of game engine programming, level design, audio middleware implementation, and art pipelines. It has been an amazing journey filled with self-discovery, challenging problems, and collaborative learning. I am deeply excited to transition into an industry where I can work alongside innovative professionals to create exciting interactive experiences.

Some of my other interests include surfing, meditation, philosophy, blockchain, and cultural exploration.
